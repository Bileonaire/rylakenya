About RYLA Kenya

Rotary Youth Leadership Awards (RYLA) is a premier leadership development program dedicated to empowering young people with essential leadership skills, boosting their self-confidence, and fostering a deep understanding of service. Organized annually at the District 9212 level (Kenya, Eritrea, Ethiopia, South Sudan), RYLA Kenya brings together participants from diverse backgrounds to engage in impactful activities, workshops, and discussions. These experiences aim to enhance leadership potential, promote teamwork, and instill a commitment to service.

RYLA provides a platform for young individuals to grow both personally and professionally while connecting with like-minded peers and mentors within the community. Each year, RYLA Kenya has evolved to address current global challenges, ensuring that participants not only develop critical leadership skills but also learn to adapt and innovate. Our programs have included a mix of workshops, interactive sessions, community service projects, and networking opportunities, all designed to inspire and equip young leaders to make a positive impact in their communities.
