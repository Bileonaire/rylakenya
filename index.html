About RYLA Kenya

Rotary Youth Leadership Awards (RYLA) is a premier leadership development program dedicated to empowering young people with essential leadership skills, boosting their self-confidence, and fostering a deep understanding of service. Organized annually at the District 9212 level (Kenya, Eritrea, Ethiopia, South Sudan), RYLA Kenya brings together participants from diverse backgrounds to engage in impactful activities, workshops, and discussions. These experiences aim to enhance leadership potential, promote teamwork, and instill a commitment to service.

RYLA provides a platform for young individuals to grow both personally and professionally while connecting with like-minded peers and mentors within the community. Each year, RYLA Kenya has evolved to address current global challenges, ensuring that participants not only develop critical leadership skills but also learn to adapt and innovate. Our programs have included a mix of workshops, interactive sessions, community service projects, and networking opportunities, all designed to inspire and equip young leaders to make a positive impact in their communities.

Our Mission

- Provide an effective training experience for selected youth and potential leaders
- Encourage leadership of youth by youth
- Encourage youth to make a difference in their communities through volunteer work and social responsibility

Upcoming Events

Mini-RYLA programs are one-day sub-programs designed to emulate the main RYLA LEAP program's activities and lessons. These condensed programs aim to broaden the impact of RYLA LEAP by reaching people who may not have the capacity to attend the full 3 days program . Similar to the main program, Mini-RYLA programs focus on empowering young leaders with essential skills and knowledge to be an innovative leader, while emphasizing the values of integrity, honesty, trustworthiness, loyalty, respect, and care.

RYLA LEAP is targeting to impact over 1000 participants in the 5 regions. A mini RYLA for interactors in high schools in Nairobi and Thika region is also set to impact over 200 interactors. This will help familiarize the interactors with Rotary Youth Leadership Awards. The programs are set to take part regionally on the following listed dates:

Coastal Region: 24th August
Mount Kenya Region : 7th September
Nakuru Region : 7th September
Western Region: 21st September
Nairobi Region : 5th October
